// 001 — MISSION BRIEF

THE PROBLEM

Jet fighters are costly and high-risk to operate. Saudi Arabia lacks a fixed-wing loyal wingman platform. An unmanned test aircraft is needed to reduce pilot risk and enable manned–unmanned teaming in combat and surveillance missions.

Our solution: KHAFI — a stealth-inspired UCAV prototype that flies ahead of the manned fighter, extending radar coverage and protecting the pilot. The F-15 pilot commands the wingman to take off, scan, detect, and return — all autonomously.

// 002 — THE AIRCRAFT

KHAFI PROTOTYPE

Blended wing body (BWB) with 1.80m wingspan and 35° sweep angle. CNC-cut EPS20 foam core wrapped in carbon fiber with epoxy — lightweight, structurally robust, and stealth-optimized.
